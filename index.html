Greetings from Placement Cell,CUCEK.Congratulations on being placed and receiving a job offer on your academia in CUCEK.As part of the placement activities and other procedures the placement cell will be accepting a small portion of your first month CTC for placement cell development fund.Kindly see the procedures and rules below to know more.

Rules and Procedure for paying the development fee

  • Those who have received placement through the CUSAT Placement cell should invariably pay the development fund.
  • The development fund payable will be fixed by the placement cell in case of each company.(The development fund will be 18% of the monthly CTC,rounded off to the nearest 100’s)
  • A student with more than 1 job offer will have to pay the development fund based on the higher CTC offered.
  • The fees should be paid at State Bank of India (SBI), PULINCUNNNU Branch,

    • Account Number : 57042777702

    Account Name: PLACEMENT CELL

    IFSC Code: SBIN0070358

    Bank: SBI PULINCUNNU

  • Students may pay the fees through direct transfer at the bank counter or through electronic transfer mention your University Registration number for identification during transfer.
  • To obtain no dues from the Placement Cell, submit a copy of the counterfoil (mention your name in the counterfoil/the receipt of online transfer) of the bank receipt along with the application.
  • All payments via Internet Banking,UPI,NEFT or Offline modes etc should be made to the above ACCOUNT and with the University Reg No. tagged to differentaite payments.
  • Electronic and Online payments should upload a copy of the payment receipt mentioning Reg No: in the link below!
  • After successful payment and verification the no dues certificate will be issued.

    • Note::No payments should be made through SBI COLLECT of CUCEK! as payments should be made to the Placement Cell ACCOUNT mentioned above.
